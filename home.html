group of companies

since 1992

Khalis Group is a market leader in Oil, Ghee, Banaspati and recycled fibre. The companies working under Khalis Group make environment friendly and hygienic products for its B2B clients as well as household consumers.

Khalis Group of Companies

Tahir Omer Industries Ltd

The company, named after two sons of Rana Iqbal Hussain, Tahir and Omer, got started in 1992. The company produces ghee and cooking oil.

Khalis Oil & Ghee Industries Ltd

With aim to reach towering heights and the use of finest technology for production of cooking oil, banaspati (vanaspati) and raw/refined canola oils.

Khalis Fibre

Khalis Fibre is the first of its kind project. The fibre produced here uses PET bottles as raw material which otherwise are not decomposed and add pollution and carbon emissions to our environment.

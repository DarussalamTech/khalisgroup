Khalis Welfare Foundation

The objectives of the society are all or any of the following:

  1. Spread the teaching & thought of great leaders, philosophers, thinkers, reformers and saints.
  2. To aware people about various social & religious issues and to fight against evils.
  3. To run/operate different types of education program/schools like formal, non-formal, and pre-school for the development of under-privileged children of the community/society.
  4. Establishment of schooling facilities for under-privileged and less fortunate families, who are desirous to impart good education to their children but are handicapped due to lack of resources.
  5. To organize and take up health, education and welfare programs for needy women and children on priority basis.
  6. To provide technical education in various fields in order to enable the students to earn their livelihood with self-respect.
  7. To organize awareness camps, health camps, workshops, campaigns and exhibitions.
  8. To affiliate itself, or associate with other institutions, establish faculties and to select faculty members and decide courses and teaching methods as well as strategies in order to ensure the most effective education programs.
  9. To promote, establish, run manage and maintain, educational institutions, research, sciences,information technology and business administration; higher level schools, academics, technical training centers and such other educational institutions as may be considered appropriate for the promotion and advancement of education in the country with affiliations to acquire the service of professors, associate professors, lecturers, teachers, scholars, management skills and other professionals , from within the country and abroad as would be needed to run and promote related educational institutions set up by the society subject however, to the permission of competent authority but not to operate itself as a university and not to act as a degree awarding institution.
  10. To set up, own, install , operate and maintain data processing centers, research centers, information centers and to utilize in any form of electronic, mechanical or other developed techniques for the purposes of advancement of learning and knowledge.

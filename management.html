Management

Profile

Khalis Group is a market leader in Oil, Ghee, Banaspati and recycled fibre.

Board of Directors

The board of Directors tries their best to bring their extensive experience and expertise in relevant industry. Following are the board members of Khalis Group:

1. Rana Iqbal Hussain (Chairman)

Rana Iqbal Hussain is the founder and the chairman of the group. He has a vast experience of international as well as local edible oil/ghee and seeds market. His entrepreneurial skills enabled Khalis Group to reach towering heights and a path to success.

2. Rana Tahir Iqbal (Director)

Rana Tahir Iqbal is the Executive Director and elder son of the Chairman. He has graduated with a business degree from King’s College London. He looks after all the operations of the group and represents the group.

3. Rana Omer Iqbal (Director)

Rana Omer Iqbal is the younger son of the chairman. He has completed his higher study from Canada.

4. Altamush Anwar Baig (Director)

Mr. Altmaush Anwar Baig is responsible for accreditation and outsourcing of the group.

Senior Management

Senior management of the Khalis Group comprises of the following members:

1. Rana Muhammad Arif (CFO)

He is the Chief Financial Officer of the group and has vast experience of more than 15 years. He is responsible for all the taxation, corporate and overall management affairs of the group.

2. Rana Muharram Ali

He is responsible for dealing with Government power resources, supply departments and other legal matters of the group.

3. Javed Khalid (Group Manager - Technical)

He is looking after all the matters of plant erections, extensions and dealing with contractors of the group using his vast experience in this area.

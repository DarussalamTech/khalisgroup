Tahir Omer Industries Limited

In the beginning of 1992, Rana Iqbal Hussain, the Chairman and Founder started this business in Sheikhupura District, and afterwards named his company after his two sons Tahir and Omer. It got registered as Tahir Omer Industries Limited (TOIL). Now TOIL has grown into a reputed name in the industry and has achieved the milestone of being in the top 100 taxpayers of Pakistan.

With an experience of over 20 years and on the basis of well governed procedures found on the highest ethical and moral practices, TOIL has instituted a good business footing in the edible oil, ghee and solvent extraction sector in Pakistan.

Now with a composition of the 3 companies the group has been named Khalis Group.

 

Philosophy

Our philosophy at group level is to provide customers with peace of mind. We are dedicated to maintain the highest standards of integrity and sound dealing in our relationship with all stakeholders, customers, intermediaries, employees, shareholders and business partners. In every course of action we seek to make a positive contribution towards community activities and are committed to perform in socially responsible manner.
